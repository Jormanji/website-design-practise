Who we are

Welcome to Smith and Co, a venerable institution in the realm of accountancy, where tradition meets innovation in the pursuit of financial excellence. Established in 1965, our firm has stood the test of time, earning a distinguished reputation for unwavering integrity, meticulous attention to detail, and unparalleled expertise in the complex world of finance.

At Smith and Co, we pride ourselves on being more than just accountants; we are trusted advisors and strategic partners dedicated to navigating the intricate landscape of financial management alongside our clients. Our commitment to excellence is deeply rooted in the rich history of our firm, which has evolved and adapted to the dynamic needs of businesses across generations.

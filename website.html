How hard is it?

When first starting out, coding can seem a bit overwhelming!

But don't worry, it's not so bad!

What do I need to become a Northcoder?

When you try to become a Northcoder, you really need to make sure you have the following things:

  • A computer that is capable of running Linux or a Macbook.

    • This is needed as Windows is less functional for coding that Linux or Mac.

  • Ability to complete the intro challenge!

    • Don't worry, this is a relaxed environment that is just here to test your understanding of what you have learned in the self-directed learning.

  • Determination and a love of problem solving

    • When coding, most of your life will be spent solving problems with your code!

Ordered steps to becoming a Northcoder

  1. Get a computer or laptop to code on. As explained further here.
  2. Go onto the Northcoders website and begin working through the learning tasks. (You can find this attached here: Northcoders.com)
  3. Apply for the entrance test using the website. Northcoders.com
  4. Complete the entrance test and choose the course you'd like to join.

What will I learn on my Northcoders course?

  • You will learn the foundations of coding.
  • You will learn how the internet works.
  • You will learn the language of JavaScript.
  • You will have the skills to be a full-stack developer
  • Finally, you will be given careers support afterwards to help you find the first job of your career.

Good luck on your journey!
